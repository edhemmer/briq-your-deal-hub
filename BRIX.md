# BRIX Product Constitution and Engineering Standard

## Authority

This document is the governing product constitution and highest product authority for BRIX. Every product decision, workflow, calculation, data model, interface, AI behavior, report, integration, test, web implementation, native iOS implementation, backend service, and administrative control must conform to it.

When legacy code, old prompts, prior documentation, implementation shortcuts, or developer assumptions conflict with this constitution, this constitution takes precedence until intentionally amended. Git history provides version control. There must not be multiple competing constitutions.

---

# 1. Executive Vision

## What BRIX Is

BRIX is a Property Deal Relationship Management platform, or PDRM, and a professional real estate investment operating system.

BRIX manages the full relationship between an investor and a property opportunity from discovery through screening, underwriting, visit, offer, contract, due diligence, financing, acquisition, operation, improvement, refinance, disposition, and archival.

BRIX is not a listing website, decorative dashboard, spreadsheet clone, mortgage calculator, generic AI chatbot, passive document repository, or disconnected collection of modules.

The property is the asset. The deal is the lifecycle. The evidence is the truth. The recommendation is the current conclusion. The investor remains the final decision maker. Everything revolves around one canonical deal.

## Vision

BRIX exists to become the most trusted real estate investment decision platform for first-time investors, experienced operators, and growing portfolios.

## Mission

Real estate investing is the practice of making decisions under uncertainty. Property data is incomplete. Market conditions change. Documents conflict. Photos hide problems. Financing terms alter outcomes. Inspections and appraisals can change the deal. Contract language can shift risk. Time spent pursuing weak opportunities has real cost.

BRIX transforms uncertainty into informed action by collecting, organizing, verifying, analyzing, and preserving the evidence connected to a property while producing deterministic calculations, explainable recommendations, professional workflows, and a complete decision history.

## North Star

> Every decision deserves evidence. Every calculation deserves certainty. Every investor deserves confidence.

## Primary Question

Every deal workspace must answer:

> What is the current state of this opportunity, what is the strongest available strategy, what evidence supports that conclusion, and what should the investor do next?

# 2. Product Philosophy

## Investor First

Everything in BRIX exists to improve the investor's decision. Every workflow, calculation, report, alert, lesson, recommendation, and control must materially improve at least one of the following:

- decision quality
- underwriting accuracy
- evidence quality
- investor understanding
- time efficiency
- field efficiency
- collaboration
- risk identification
- portfolio awareness
- confidence through transparency

If a capability does not improve one of these outcomes, it does not belong in BRIX.

## Truth Above Convenience

Accuracy takes precedence over convenience, speed, appearance, automation, or marketing.

Every material value must be classified as one of:

- Confirmed Fact
- User-Entered Fact
- External Estimate
- User Assumption
- System
