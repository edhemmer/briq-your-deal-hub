# BRIX

## Governing Authority

BRIX.md is the governing product constitution and highest product authority for this repository.

Every architecture decision, workflow, calculation, user interface, native iOS implementation, data model, AI behavior, document, report, integration, test, and operational control must conform to this constitution.

If legacy code, prior prompts, comments, mocks, screenshots, or implementation choices conflict with BRIX.md, BRIX.md takes precedence until it is intentionally amended.

BRIX must be built in dependency order. New ideas are added to the roadmap and implemented when the required foundations are ready. Feature requests must not fragment the product, create competing data models, duplicate calculations, or interrupt a stable build sequence.

---

## Product Identity

BRIX is a Property Deal Relationship Management platform, or PDRM, and a real estate investment operating system.

BRIX manages the full relationship between an investor and a property opportunity from discovery through qualification, underwriting, research, visit, negotiation, offer, contract, due diligence, financing, closing, operation, improvement, refinance, disposition, and exit.

BRIX is not a listing website, generic dashboard, AI chatbot, mortgage calculator, document repository, inspection replacement, or disconnected set of modules.

The property is the asset.

The deal is the lifecycle.

The evidence is the basis.

The underwriting is deterministic.

The recommendation is explainable.

The decision history is permanent.

The central question BRIX must answer is:

> What is the current state of this opportunity, what is the strongest available strategy, what evidence supports the conclusion, what remains uncertain, and what should the investor do next?
