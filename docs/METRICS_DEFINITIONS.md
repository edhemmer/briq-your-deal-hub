# BRIQ Metrics & KPI Definitions

## Financial Metrics

### Cap Rate (Capitalization Rate)
```
Cap Rate = (Net Operating Income / Purchase Price) × 100
```
- **Target:** >8% for rental properties
- **Signal:** Property income yield independent of financing

### Cash-on-Cash Return (CoC)
```
CoC = (Annual Cash Flow / Total Cash Invested) × 100
```
- **Target:** >10% for most investors
- **Signal:** Return on actual cash invested

### Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR)
```
DSCR = Net Operating Income / Annual Debt Service
```
- **Target:** >1.25 for lender approval
- **Signal:** Ability to cover loan payments

### Monthly Cash Flow
```
Cash Flow = Gross Rent - (Mortgage + Operating Expenses)
```
- **Target:** Positive, >$200/unit
- **Signal:** Ongoing profitability

## Intelligence Scores

### Deal Intelligence Score (0-100)
Composite score combining:
- Financial metrics (40% weight)
- Market conditions (30% weight)
- Risk factors (20% weight)
- Strategy fit (10% weight)

| Score Range | Rating | Action |
|-------------|--------|--------|
| 80-100 | Excellent | Strong buy signal |
| 60-79 | Good | Proceed with due diligence |
| 40-59 | Fair | Caution advised |
| 0-39 | Poor | Pass recommended |

### Market Strength Score
Combines:
- Price appreciation trends
- Rent growth
- Days on market
- Supply/demand balance

### Demand Pressure Score
Measures:
- Population growth
- Job growth
- Inventory levels
- Absorption rate

### Crime Risk Signal (0-10)
- **0-3:** Low risk
- **4-6:** Moderate risk
- **7-10:** High risk

## Business Metrics

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
```
CAC = Total Marketing Spend / New Customers Acquired
```

### Lifetime Value (LTV)
```
LTV = ARPU × Average Customer Lifespan (months)
```

### Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
```
MRR = Number of Paying Subscribers × Monthly Price
```

### Churn Rate
```
Churn = Customers Lost / Customers at Start of Period
```
