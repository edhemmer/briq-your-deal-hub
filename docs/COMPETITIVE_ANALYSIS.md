## Competitor Profiles

### DealCheck
- **Price:** $15/month (Pro)
- **Strength:** Affordable, mobile app
- **Weakness:** Limited market data, no AI extraction
- **BRIQ Advantage:** 7-engine intelligence vs basic calculations

### BiggerPockets Calculators
- **Price:** Free (with membership)
- **Strength:** Large community, brand recognition
- **Weakness:** Basic functionality, no scoring
- **BRIQ Advantage:** Comprehensive analysis, AI extraction

### Excel/Sheets Templates
- **Price:** Free
- **Strength:** Flexibility, familiarity
- **Weakness:** Manual, error-prone, no market data
- **BRIQ Advantage:** Automation, intelligence, consistency

### CoStar/Yardi
- **Price:** $500-2,000+/month
- **Strength:** Enterprise data, MLS integration
- **Weakness:** Price prohibitive for individuals
- **BRIQ Advantage:** 90% lower cost, purpose-built for deal analysis

## BRIQ Differentiation

### Primary Differentiators

1. **AI-Powered Data Entry**
   - Paste listing text → structured data in seconds
   - Screenshot upload → automatic extraction
   - No competitor offers this at our price point

2. **Seven-Engine Intelligence**
   - Integrated analysis across financial, market, and risk dimensions
   - Composite scoring with confidence levels
   - Competitors offer 1-2 dimensions at most

3. **Strategy Fit Alignment**
   - Automatic evaluation against Buy & Hold, Flip, BRRRR, Wholesale
   - No competitor provides strategy-specific recommendations

4. **Stress Testing**
   - Scenario modeling for adverse conditions
   - Break-even analysis
   - Typically enterprise-only feature
