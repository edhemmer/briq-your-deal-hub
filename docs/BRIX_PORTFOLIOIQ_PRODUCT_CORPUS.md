# BRIX PortfolioIQ Product Corpus v1.0

## Purpose

PortfolioIQ is the asset intelligence engine of BRIX.

Its purpose is to help users manage, monitor, optimize, and grow their real estate portfolio after acquisition. PortfolioIQ transforms BRIX from an acquisition platform into a complete real estate operating system.

PortfolioIQ begins when a property is acquired.

## User Goal

PortfolioIQ answers: **How is my investment performing?**

Users should understand:

- Current portfolio value
- Equity position
- Cash flow performance
- Asset performance
- Portfolio performance
- Risk exposure
- Future opportunities

## Core Principle

Most real estate software ends at closing. BRIX continues after closing.

PortfolioIQ ensures users remain engaged throughout the entire ownership lifecycle.

## Asset Creation

Properties enter PortfolioIQ through:

PipelineIQ status = Closed -> Create Asset -> PortfolioIQ Record

No duplicate entry. All acquisition history remains attached to the asset.

## Asset Dashboard

- Property information

PortfolioIQ tracks:
