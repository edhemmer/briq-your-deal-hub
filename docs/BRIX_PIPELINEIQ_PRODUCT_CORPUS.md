# BRIX PipelineIQ Product Corpus v1.0

## Purpose

PipelineIQ is the workflow intelligence engine of BRIX.

Its purpose is to manage opportunities from discovery through acquisition. PipelineIQ ensures no opportunity is lost, forgotten, duplicated, or left without clear next actions.

PipelineIQ is not underwriting. PipelineIQ is not portfolio management. PipelineIQ manages acquisition execution.

## User Goal

PipelineIQ answers: **Where does this opportunity stand right now?**

Users should instantly understand:

- Current status
- Next action
- Outstanding tasks
- Responsible party
- Important deadlines
- Probability of closing

## Opportunity Lifecycle

Default stages:

- New Opportunity
- Reviewing
- Underwriting
- Offer Strategy
- Offer Drafted
- Offer Submitted
- Negotiating
- Under Contract
- Due Diligence
- Closing
- Closed
- Rejected

## Pipeline Views

Supported views:

- Kanban Board
- List View
- Table View
- Calendar View

Kanban is the default view.

## Tasks

Every opportunity supports tasks with:

- Due dates
- Priority levels
- Status
- Notes
- Assignments

Example tasks:

- Call listing agent
- Schedule showing
- Run DealIQ analysis
- Verify taxes
- Obtain insurance quote
- Schedule inspection
- Review title commitment
- Submit offer

## Notes & Activity

Every opportunity maintains an activity timeline tracking:

- Notes
- Calls
- Emails
- Status changes
- Offer submissions
- Document uploads
- Team activity

Every action should be traceable.

## Opportunity Health Score

Every pipeline record receives a health score to identify opportunities at risk of being neglected.

Factors may include:

- Task completion
- Pipeline activity
- Days inactive
- Missing data
- Pending deadlines

## Integrations

PipelineIQ consumes data from:

- FindIQ for opportunity creation
- DealIQ for analysis results
- OfferIQ for offer generation
- PortfolioIQ for closed acquisitions

PipelineIQ acts as the operational hub connecting all BRIX modules.

## Automation

Automation should reduce manual work.

Examples:

- Auto-create task after DealIQ analysis
- Auto-remind users of deadlines
- Auto-notify when offers remain unanswered
- Auto-move opportunities based on workflow events
- Auto-create PortfolioIQ asset after closing

## Success Metric

PipelineIQ succeeds when users can answer:

**What opportunities am I pursuing, what needs attention next, and what is the likelihood of closing?**
